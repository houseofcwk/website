## SPRAYCATION Mural Tour 2025: Building an Experiential Campaign While in Motion

**Video:** YouTube `indEyPSLvlI` (SPRAYCATION Mural Tour 2025 Kick-off)

SPRAYCATION is a female-led experiential art movement built around large-scale public murals, storytelling, and creative action.

The tour for 2025 centered on a single message: You Are Magic, a call to reclaim creative power, occupy public space, and choose yourself.

CWK. began working with Zuzu in January 2025.
The tour launched in June and ran through November.

---

## The Work

CWK.'s role was to build the brand foundation and operational backbone while the tour was already in motion.

That included:

- Centering the entire tour around a single narrative
- Hiring and coordinating an operations team in real time
- Creating backend systems for sales, customer service, and partners
- Designing repeatable processes and templates
- Supporting Zuzu in the mural reveal / celebration events in select cities

Everything was built under live conditions.

**Image:** `Client_Proof/You_Are_Magic_05-9099321.png`

---

## The Result

SPRAYCATION moved from an ambitious art idea to a repeatable, fundable experiential brand.

The tour proved:

- The message holds across cities
- The model works beyond one-off murals
- The experience can be packaged, sold, and scaled

2024 proved the concept.
2025 refined the narrative.
2026 is built for scale.
