## Question 1 of 4: Current State

**Which best describes how you feel about your business right now?**

1. **Overwhelmed** - I'm drowning. I can't keep up with everything coming at me.
2. **Frustrated** - I'm working so hard, but nothing seems to be stacking or building momentum.
3. **Exhausted** - I'm tired of rebuilding the same momentum every week. It feels like Groundhog Day.
4. **Skeptical** - I've tried so many things already. Why would this be any different?
5. **Ready** - I know exactly what's broken. I just need the right partner to help me fix it.

## Question 2 of 4: Revenue Situation

**Which best describes your revenue situation right now?**

1. **Volatile Revenue** - $10k one month, $40k the next. I have no idea what's coming in or when.
2. **Stuck at a Ceiling** - I'm working harder than ever but making the same amount as last year, or less.
3. **Underpricing** - I know I'm undercharging, but I'm scared that raising prices will make me lose clients.
4. **Low Margins** - I'm bringing in revenue, but after expenses, there's almost nothing left.
5. **Founder-Dependent** - If I stop working, the revenue stops. I can't take time off without losing deals.

## Question 3 of 4: Systems

**Which best describes your current systems situation?**

1. **Scattered Systems** - My tools, content, and client info are spread across a dozen places. It's chaos.
2. **Manual Processes** - Everything depends on me remembering to do it. There's no automation or system.
3. **No Compounding IP** - I keep recreating the same assets, proof, and content from scratch every single time.
4. **Fragile Authority** - I can't find my case studies or proof fast enough when I need them on sales calls.
5. **Leaking Deals** - Warm leads go cold because I drop the ball on follow-ups. I know it's costing me revenue.

## Question 4 of 4: Team

**Which best describes your current team situation?**

1. **Solo (No Team)** - It's just me. I can't afford to hire anyone yet, so I'm doing everything myself.
2. **Has VAs (Can't Delegate)** - I have help, but they can't do anything without me explaining it in detail every single time.
3. **Team Exists (No Systems)** - My team is talented, but we don't have clear processes, so everything feels chaotic.
4. **Team + Systems (Messy)** - We have systems, but they're scattered and confusing. No one knows where anything actually is.
5. **Ready to Scale** - We have a team and some systems in place. We just need to tighten the infrastructure to scale.
